About

7/2020

GMU Senior Liberal Arts student seeking further instruction in English, Folklore, and related topics

My life is awhirl with enough arts and crafts to distract me from living with my parents and brothers during the COVID-19 crisis.

I have a NOVA Associates Degree in Liberal Arts and often take classes to expand my knowledge of the world. My current studies are in Creative Writing, Editing, Web Design, History, and Folklore.

See me at My Account on ArchiveOfOurOwn.org, a Hugo winning site by the Organization for Transformative Works™.

My account contains transformative works of fiction, including one series and a comedy.

I also do Grammar and Spelling editing for short fantasy works and teach piano at Piano Express™.
